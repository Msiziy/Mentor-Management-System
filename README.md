# Mentor Management System

## Project Overview

The **Mentor Management System** is a software application developed to improve the process of assigning mentors to students in an academic environment. The system ensures that students are paired with mentors based on their **school or department**, rather than random allocation.

---

## Problem Statement

In many institutions, students—especially first years—often struggle to identify or connect with their assigned mentors. This lack of connection can make it difficult for students to adapt to the academic environment and access necessary guidance.

---

## Proposed Solution

The Mentor Management System addresses this issue by:

- Automatically assigning students to mentors based on their **school/department**
- Providing a structured platform where students can easily identify and contact their mentors
- Ensuring a more organized and efficient mentoring process

---

## How It Works

1. Users register and log into the system
2. Admin adds mentors and students into the system
3. The system assigns students to mentors based on matching school/department
4. Students can view their assigned mentors
5. Mentors can view and interact with their assigned students

---

##  Conclusion

The Mentor Management System improves the mentoring process by ensuring proper alignment between students and mentors. It enhances communication, promotes student support, and helps new students integrate more easily into the academic environment.
